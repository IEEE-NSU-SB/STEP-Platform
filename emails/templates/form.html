Student Transition and Elevation Partnership

IEEE NSU Student Branch is looking for passionate and enthusiastic members as Team Volunteers for its respective Teams If you are interested in learning and developing your technical skills, and most importantly, if you want to get a full-fledged volunteering experience, then fill out the form without hesitation. Check out the eligibility requirements before applying.

Teams Available:

  1. Content Writing and Publications Team
  2. Events and Management Team
  3. Finance and Corporate Team
  4. Graphics Team
  5. Logistics and Operations Team
  6. Media Team
  7. Membership Development Team
  8. Public Relations Team
  9. Promotions Team
  10. Website Development Team

Team Volunteers will have the opportunity to work for their desired teams while being supervised by the In-charges. They will be supervised by the In-charges while gaining information and skills through task-based learning.

Application Conditions for Team Volunteers:

  • Candidates must have an active IEEE membership.
  • Candidates can apply for maximum 5 teams and minimum 2 teams.   

Steps to Apply:

All the applicants must complete the following:

  • Fill up the Application Form.
  • Must attend all the stages of the process announced by the Selection Committee. 
  • Candidates have to sit for a Viva Session after the completion and submission of the form.
  • Absence of any required information will automatically invalidate your application.

Deadline: 7th May, 2025; 10:00 PM
